Mathematics

Matrix is a responsible for various events and lecture series related to Mathematics. This ensures full development of students as they learn about the real time applications of their theoritical knowledge which they have gained during their curriculum. Also Lecture series by some of the greatest minds in the world, allow our students to interact with such immense personalities and ponder to achieve a great research breakthrough. Various Events such as LaTeX workshops and other relevant resourceful workshops help our research community to present a fruitful publication of their research projects. Matrix is planning to conduct a workshop on cryptography and encryption techniques.

Computer Science

Coding skills help a person in various scenarios of their professional life. Matrix is responsible to build up this skill by conducting events such as Coding Competitions and Hackathons. Various workshops on web development, network management, understanding our operating system etc. are also conducted by Matrix.

Financial Engineering

Matrix conduct events such a Virtual Stock Market (VSM), which is a very innovative idea for understanding the stock exchange. Matrix is responsible for conducting various workshops such as Portfolio Management to help the community by giving an exposure of the outside exchange techniques.
