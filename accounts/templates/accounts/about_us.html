About

MATRIX Student Body, Department of Mathematics, IIT Guwahati, was founded in 2008 with a vision to provide exposure for applying the mathematics and computing knowledge to solve the real life technical problems by organizing lectures, seminars, workshops and events.

Dimension

Dimension is the annual summit of MATRIX. Wherein we invite dignitaries to visit our campus and address the students, about their industrial experiences, their research projects etc. Previously Dimension has had lecture series by eminent scientists from various fields such as Cryptography, Fractals, Artificial Intelligence etc.

Anantha

The Annual Magazine published by MATRIX
This magazine, which contains a wide range of interesting articles contributed by different people, related to Latest Mathematical Research, Internships' Experiences, and many more interesting articles revolving around student life at IITG, is a great medium to share ideas, innovations and experiences.
