About

MATRIX Student Body, Department of Mathematics, IIT Guwahati, was founded in 2008 with a vision to provide exposure for applying the mathematics and computing knowledge to solve the real life technical problems by organizing lectures, seminars and workshops on topics of general interest and special lectures on specific topics of interest.

Dimension

Dimension is the annual festival of IITG Mathematics Society (MATRIX). Many important personalities, visit our campus to address the audience, about their industrial experiences, research projects. Lecture series by eminent scientists from various fields such as Cryptography, Fractals, Artificial Intelligence are held during Dimension.

Anantha

The Annual Magazine published by MATRIX
Publication of thoughts is very important for a person to improve its profile. Matrix helps the community, by issuing its magazine “Anantha”, which contains a wide range of interesting articles contribute by different people, related to Latest Mathematical Research, Experience of Internships, and many more.
