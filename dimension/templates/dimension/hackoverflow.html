About the Event

HackOverFlow is a unique Data Science Hackathon event organized by MATRIX, Student Body, Department of Mathematics, IIT Guwahati and Consulting & Analytics Club, IIT Guwahati. A first of its kind event at IIT Guwahati, HackOverFlow will see participation from the North East and will focus on developing Data Science solutions for prominent industry challenges. The best teams will be invited to 3 month Summer Camp by Probyto at IIT Guwahati to develop Minimal Viable Prototypes(MVPs).

About the Organizers

HackOverFlow is a unique event designed to spread awareness about Data Science and encourage new talent to join the field. This event is being organised at IIT Guwahati as a joint initiative of Matrix IIT Guwahati, Consulting Club IIT Guwahati and Probyto, an independent and active organization in the field of data Science.

Event Vision

At HackOverFlow, we want to create Guwahati as a vibrant, self-sustaining, innovation-driven city. We invite original, innovative ideas from participants, that have the potential to transform lives and we want to help shape these ideas into finished products that can make an impact.

Problems



Smart Customer Geo-Intelligence

Demographic attributes of prospective customers of your business are not always known for un-registered users. Geo-location of the customer landing in your platform gives us fair idea for what he/she might be looking for in the platform. There are multiple sources of aggregated demographic information to ascertain some features of the customer to help design a customized web-pages or campaign. The incoming traffic of website can provide the IP address of the machine and using that we can infer the location of machine. The location then can help us gather data about aggregated features from hundreds of sources and help design the customized experience for each customer.

Cyber Security

Internet is becoming diverse and complicated than ever before. Our financial life to social life is driven by internet. This makes it important to safeguard the cyber world from un-wanted malicious agents. Probyto is getting equipped to use machine learning algorithms for cyber security which also connects with application, information and network security. The recent attacks of ransomware, botnets, and other vectors has been increasing the organizational risk and they are looking for sophisticated machine learning algorithms for anomaly detection as an alternative to the ineffective static signature based approaches.

Robotic Process Automation

Robotic process automation (RPA) is the application of technology that helps company to configure systems to capture, interpret and make decision for low level tasks such as scheduling, monitoring, data entry and many more. RPA are typically set of methodologies and processes to automate lower level jobs based on certain rules. One of the applications of RPA is helping companies to digitize their paper based forms and documents. With advent of technology and competent business environments, it is important for companies to have all their information in digital format while the business process still relies on paper based assets. OCR machines are costly and come along with hardware. The advent in machine learning efforts can turn any scanned copy of such assets into digital form.

FAQs

What to bring?

  • Cellphone and charger

  • Extra clothes
Will there be a place to sleep?
Yes, we will be providing accomodation for all participants.
What if I don’t have a team?
You can register individually. We will create random teams of the individual participants at the venue itself.
What if I don’t have a project or idea?
There will be multiple challenges and ideas for you to choose from. You can work on those and ideate a unique solution for it.
Should I bring extra hardware?
Yes, you may bring additional hardware if you have a project in mind.
What should I prepare prior to the event?
It’s a good idea to setup your computer with some development software such as an IDE (i.e. Android Studio, XCode), a version control tool (i.e. Git). You don't want to spend a large chunk of time downloading software at the event, do it beforehand!
Should I start my project before the hackathon?
No. All work on projects should be done at the event. You can read up on different APIs and frameworks but all code should be written during the hackathon.
